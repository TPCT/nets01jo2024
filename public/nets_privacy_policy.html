NETS Privacy Policy

 

Introduction

At NETS we believe that only those you want to share your information with should receive your details.  You are the one in control of your data and no one else, and this is how we built our application.

The document here below explains how we collect, apply and maintain your data; in addition, it details your choices regarding use, maintenance and access of your personal record.

NETS is the best way to exchange and keep your contacts updated.  Not only is it easy and seamless, it also allows you to link those you meet with your calendar for better tracking and connection.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our electronic contact exchange application (the "Application").

 

What Personal Information Do We Collect?

We collect the following personal information from you when you use the Application:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your company name
  • Your job title
  • Your industry
  • Your location

We also collect information about your interactions with the Application, such as the contacts you add, the events you attend, and the places you visit.

 

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

To provide and improve the Application

To send you marketing communications, if you have opted in to receive them

To comply with applicable laws and regulations

 

How Do We Share Your Personal Information?

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your consent. We may share your personal information with third parties to provide and improve the Application, such as our payment processors and customer support providers. We may also share your personal information with law enforcement or other government officials if we are required to do so by law.

 

How Do We Protect Your Personal Information?

We take steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These steps include:

Using industry-standard security measures, such as firewalls and encryption

Limiting access to your personal information to authorized employees and contractors

Requiring employees and contractors to sign confidentiality agreements

Conducting regular security audits

 

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

The right to access your personal information

The right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information

The right to have your personal information deleted

The right to object to the processing of your personal information

The right to restrict the processing of your personal information

The right to data portability

The right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information

 

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at nets@dot.jo

 

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any significant changes, we will notify you by email or through the Application.

Contact Us
